Instructions: Enter your username as it appears on swgoh.gg and hit submit.
To assign a mod to a table select a colored radio button and then click on the row for the mod you want to assign.
Sorting: Click a column header to sort by that value, click a second header for secondary sorting. e.g. Click Spd twice followed by Slot to find your fastest mods by slot.
